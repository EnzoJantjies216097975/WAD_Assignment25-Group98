Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

General Questions

University Timetable Manager (UTM) is a web-based application that helps students create, manage, and optimize their academic schedules. It features an intuitive drag-and-drop interface, automatic conflict detection, and cloud synchronization.

Yes! UTM is completely free for all students. There are no hidden fees, premium features, or subscription costs. We believe every student should have access to quality scheduling tools.

No! UTM is a web application that runs entirely in your browser. Simply visit the website and start planning. No downloads or installations required.

UTM works best on modern browsers including Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Safari, and Microsoft Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated to the latest version.

Account & Security

Click the "Get Started" or "Register" button, enter your details (username, email, password), agree to the terms, and click "Create Account". You'll be logged in automatically!

Yes! We take security seriously. Your password is encrypted using industry-standard hashing, and all data transmission is secure. We never share your personal information with third parties.

Yes, with limited functionality. You can use LocalStorage mode to create schedules that are saved only to your browser. However, creating an account enables cloud sync, sharing, and access from multiple devices.

On the login page, click "Forgot password?" and enter your email address. You'll receive instructions to reset your password.

Using the App

Browse or search for courses in the left sidebar, then drag and drop them onto your timetable grid at the desired time slot. The system will automatically snap courses into place.

UTM automatically detects conflicts and displays visual warnings (red borders, alerts). The conflict indicator in the toolbar shows the number of conflicts. Simply drag one of the conflicting courses to a different time slot to resolve.

Yes! Create unlimited schedule versions to compare different options. This is perfect for testing different course combinations or planning backup schedules.

Open any schedule and click the "Share" button. A unique link will be generated that you can copy and send to anyone. They can view your schedule without creating an account.

Yes! With LocalStorage enabled, you can view and edit previously loaded schedules without an internet connection. Changes will sync when you reconnect.

Features & Functionality

Troubleshooting

First, check your internet connection. Try refreshing the page and attempting to save again. If the problem persists, try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser. Contact support if issues continue.

Ensure you're using a supported browser with JavaScript enabled. Try refreshing the page. On touchscreen devices, press and hold to initiate dragging.

If you're logged in, your schedules are saved to the cloud and can be accessed from "My Schedules". If using LocalStorage mode, schedules are tied to your browser - clearing browser data will delete them.
