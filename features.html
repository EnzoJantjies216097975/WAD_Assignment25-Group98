Features

Everything you need to manage your academic schedule effectively

Drag & Drop Interface

Our intuitive drag-and-drop interface makes schedule creation effortless. Simply browse available courses and drag them directly onto your timetable grid. Visual feedback shows you exactly where courses will be placed, and the system automatically snaps them into the correct time slots.

  • No complex forms to fill out
  • Instant visual feedback
  • Easy to rearrange courses
  • Touch-friendly for tablets
Drag & Drop Demo

Intelligent Conflict Detection

Never worry about scheduling conflicts again. Our smart conflict detection system automatically identifies overlapping classes, time conflicts, and scheduling issues in real-time. Get instant alerts and visual indicators when conflicts arise.

  • Real-time conflict checking
  • Visual conflict indicators
  • Automatic time overlap detection
  • Alternative time suggestions
Conflict Alert

Cloud Synchronization

Your schedules are automatically saved to the cloud, ensuring you never lose your data. Access your timetables from any device - desktop, tablet, or smartphone. Start planning on your laptop and continue on your phone.

  • Automatic cloud backup
  • Cross-device synchronization
  • Offline mode with local storage
  • Secure data encryption
Cloud Sync

Easy Schedule Sharing

Collaborate with classmates by sharing your schedules. Generate unique sharing links that allow others to view your timetable. Perfect for coordinating study groups, finding common free time, or planning group projects.

  • One-click share links
  • View-only permissions
  • No account required to view
  • Social media integration
Share Link

Multiple Schedule Versions

Create and compare multiple schedule variations before committing. Experiment with different course combinations, compare workload distributions, and find the perfect balance for your semester.

  • Unlimited schedule versions
  • Side-by-side comparison
  • Easy version switching
  • Version history tracking
Multiple Versions

Mobile Responsive Design

Fully optimized for all screen sizes. Whether you're using a desktop computer, tablet, or smartphone, the interface adapts seamlessly to provide the best user experience on every device.

  • Mobile-first design
  • Touch-optimized controls
  • Works on all devices
  • No app installation needed
Responsive

Export & Print Options

Export your schedules in multiple formats for offline use. Generate PDFs for printing, create images for sharing, or download JSON data for backup. Perfect for keeping physical copies or integrating with other tools.

  • PDF export for printing
  • Image export (PNG/JPG)
  • JSON data export
  • CSV for spreadsheets
Export Options

Course Search & Filter

Quickly find the courses you need with our powerful search and filter system. Search by course code, name, or instructor. Filter by department, credits, or availability to narrow down your options.

  • Instant search results
  • Multiple filter options
  • Department categorization
  • Custom course creation
